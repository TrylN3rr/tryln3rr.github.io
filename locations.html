Our Offices

We have two convenient locations to serve you. 
Tap or click an office address below for directions from your current location to us.

alameda

3533 s. alameda st.
sloan bldg., suite 303
corpus christi, tx 78411

361.853.3222
361.853.7311

Our Alameda office is located in the Sloan medical building behind the Driscoll Children’s rehabilitation center on south alameda.
our offices are on the third floor.

the easiest way to reach us is to enter driscoll via the 3rd entrance (the driscoll way/sinclair), continue to parking garage until 3rd floor, park in one of the designated parking spaces for "ABC patients" conveniently located next to the entrance to sloan building.

?In the event that your child’s medical condition exceeds the treatment that ABC can provide, Driscoll Children's Hospital Emergency Room is located in front of our building.?

saratoga

5945 saratoga blvd.
suite c
corpus christi, tx 78414

 361.853.3222
 361.980.3619

Our Saratoga office is located in the driscoll children’s south plaza near the intersection of saratoga and south staples.
our main entrance is on the east side of the building.

?In the event that your child’s medical condition exceeds the treatment that ABC can provide, Driscoll Children's Urgent Care is located in the same building?
