Welcome to ABC Pediatrics!

We are excited that you have chosen us as your child’s healthcare provider. We are confident you have made the right decision. ABC Pediatrics has been around for almost 30 years serving the community of South Texas. We have an all physician practice. Your child will be seen by their primary physician at all well visits to allow continuity of care for your child as they grow through their pediatric stages. As we continue to grow, we always keep the best interest in mind for our patients and our local community. 

Our Mission

To offer the best medical home for your child that includes preventative care and up to date evidence based management of acute illnesses and chronic illnesses.

our commitment

continuity of care/physician care

We are committed to offering an All Physician practice, which means your child will only see a physician at each well and sick visit. We offer after hour call so that if you ever have a concern or question regarding your child you will be able to speak with the on-call physician after hours. We are committed to providing continuity of care by scheduling all your child’s visits with their primary care provider.

communication

We believe that communication is vitally important to exceptional healthcare. Your child’s health is extremely important to us. We want to communicate to you your child’s healthcare needs during each visit. You will also have access to our website and portal to be able to access visit summaries after each visit, immunization records, medications prescribed, and growth charts. You can contact the office during business hours and leave a message with the receptionist for our in house physicians. You are also able to message your doctor through our portal for any non-urgent questions.

privacy

Your privacy and the privacy of your child’s healthcare information is very important to us. We will not disclose your child’s medical record unless you direct us to do so, or unless the law authorizes or compels us to do so. We follow Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act guidelines to ensure your child’s medical information is protected.

Health information: You may access your child’s medical information regarding visits, growth charts, immunization record, medications through our portal at ?. Being up to date on your child’s medical needs help you be an advocate for your child’s health.

Quality of Care: We strive to offer your child with the highest quality of care and focus on best practice medicine. We believe that quality of care involves preventative care as well and evaluate at each well or sick visit how we can best prevent illnesses down the road or complications from illnesses.
